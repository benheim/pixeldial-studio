## Top 3 Picks

### 1) PixelDial Studio
- Most balanced: memorable, professional, and clearly web-related.
- “Pixel” + “Dial” hits both design and dial-up nostalgia without feeling gimmicky.
- Works well for broader client types (music, fashion, creative studios).

### 2) NeonGuestbook
- Instantly communicates internet nostalgia to anyone who remembers early web culture.
- Distinctive and emotionally sticky.
- Great if you want a more playful/creative positioning.

### 3) StarBlink
- Short, premium, and versatile.
- Still feels Y2K/retro-futurist without boxing the brand into a novelty niche.
- Scales well if services expand beyond landing pages.
